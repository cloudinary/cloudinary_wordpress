Connect to Cloudinary

You need to connect your Cloudinary account to WordPress by adding your unique connection string. See below for where to find this.

Where to find my connection string?

After creating your Cloudinary account:

  1. Open your Cloudinary Dashboard
  2. At the top of the dashboard you will find the Account Details section
  3. Copy the API Environment variable
